Aceing Autism

Aceing Autism was an organization I got involved with through tennis when I was in high school. The organization provides an outlet for Autistic children to come out and enjoy the amazing game of tennis. Sometimes kids with Autism have a hard time with social interaction, this program allows for these kids to be surrounded by others who will motivate them and make them feel comfortable on the court. The program is a 1-hour long clinic that is essentially oriented whichever way the kids are feeling. If they want to try matches, we will let them try matches, if they just want to hit the ball back and forth with one of us coaches, we will certainly provide them with that. The whole experience of this program has been amazing. It allowed me to connect with amazing kids that I might have never got the chance to meet.

Boys & Girls Club

The Boys & Girls Club was one of the first organizations I got involved with as a kid. Although I have not taken the chance to volunteer as a mentor or other variation, I would love to take the leap one day. They offer such a great community for younger kids to meet friends and grow as a person. They do currently have a branch here in College Station / Bryan that I plan to look into soon. In essence, they offer kids from diverse backgrounds the chance to build friendships in various activities and also offer extensive educational resources. The Boys and Girls Club has a huge heart for children in need, it was one of their biggest pushes. In all, this organization does amazing things for the youth, feel free to check out their website below.
